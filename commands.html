Bot Commands
Here are the bot commands!
Bot Prefix: s! or S!
Admin:
ban

Bans a user

Usage: s!ban (user)

kick

Kicks a user

Usage: s!kick (user)

Bot Stuff:
invite

Shows invite info, like server and JustABot invites

Usage: s!invite

Fun:
choose

Chooses between multiple choices

Usage: s!choose (choice, choice etc..)

gay

Are you gay?

Usage: s!gay (user)

spam

Spams a text for a certain amount of times

Usage: s!spam (amount) (text)

Math:
add

Adds two numbers together

Usage: s!add (1st number) (2nd number)

divide

Divides two numbers

Usage: s!divide (1st number) (2nd number)

multiply

Multiplies two numbers together

Usage: s!multiply (1st number) (2nd number)

subtract

Subtracts two numbers

Usage: s!add (1st number) (2nd number)

Music:
join

Makes the bot join your voice channel

Usage: s!join *(channel)

pause

Pauses the currently played song

Usage: s!pause

play

Plays a song

Usage: s!play (song)

playing

Shows info about the currently played song

Usage: s!playing

resume

Resumes the currently played song

Usage: s!resume

skip

Vote to skip a song. The song requester can automatically skip

Usage: s!skip

stop

Stops playing audio and leaves the voice channel

Usage: s!stop

volume

Sets the volume of the currently playing song

Usage: s!volume (value)

Owner Only:
shutdown

Shuts down JustABot

Usage: s!shutdown
